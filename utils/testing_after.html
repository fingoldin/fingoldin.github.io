Now the real sequences begin!

On the next screen you will be shown 50 consecutive sequences, each of 10 ticket prices, just as you were shown before.

You will be awarded a bonus in money that depends on your performance in these next 50 sequences. For
  • Picking the cheapest ticket you will get 2 points
  • Picking the second cheapest ticket you will get 1 point
  • Otherwise you will get no points

For every point you will recieve $0.025.
