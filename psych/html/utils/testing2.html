Part 4: Test Phase
On the next screen you will be shown 50 consecutive sequences, each of 10 ticket prices, just as in the first of the experiment.

Your total amount of points will be increased according to the same rules as before:
  • Picking the cheapest ticket you will get 3 points
  • Picking the second cheapest ticket you will get 2 point
  • Picking the third, forth or fifth cheapest ticket you will get 1 point
  • Otherwise you will get no points

We remind you again that at the end your bonus will be proportional to your points, e.g. if you get all the points (300 points) you get $5.00, if you get half the points (75 points) you get $2.50.
